# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but focus on facts and reproducible evidence.
- Accept feedback gracefully.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discrimination based on personal characteristics.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Report violations to `contact@ovumcy.com`.
Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or ban contributors for violations.
